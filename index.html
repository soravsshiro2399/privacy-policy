01Introduction

Team DD develops and publishes mobile applications and games ("Apps"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our Apps. By downloading, installing, or using any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

02Information We Collect

Depending on the App and how you use it, we may collect:

  • Device information — device model, operating system, language, and advertising identifiers (e.g. IDFA, GAID)
  • Usage data — in-app activity, gameplay progress, session length, and feature interactions, used to improve our products
  • Diagnostic data — crash reports and performance logs
  • Approximate location — derived from IP address or device settings, where applicable
  • Information you provide directly — such as a display name or content submitted through support or feedback channels

We do not knowingly collect more information than is reasonably necessary to operate, maintain, and improve our Apps.

03In-App Advertising

Our Apps may display advertisements served by third-party advertising networks. These partners may use device identifiers and other technical data to deliver and measure ads.

We may work with industry-standard advertising and analytics partners, which may include providers such as Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, Unity Ads, AppLovin, and ironSource, among others. Each partner processes data according to its own privacy policy. You can review Google's approach at policies.google.com/privacy, and manage ad personalization through your device's privacy settings at any time (for example, "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

04In-App Purchases

Some Apps offer optional in-app purchases, such as virtual currency, feature unlocks, or ad removal. All payments are processed directly by the applicable app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) under their respective terms and privacy policies. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We only receive confirmation of a completed transaction in order to unlock the corresponding content in the App.

05How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Advertising networks, for ad delivery and measurement
  • Analytics providers, to help us understand app usage and improve performance
  • Cloud and backend service providers, to operate features such as save data and leaderboards
  • Law enforcement or regulators, where required by applicable law

We do not sell your personal information for monetary consideration.

06Device Permissions

Depending on the App's features, we may request permissions such as notifications, storage access, camera, or microphone. You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings; some features may not function correctly if a required permission is denied.

07Children's Privacy

Our Apps are designed to be suitable for a general audience, including children. We do not knowingly collect more personal information from children than is reasonably necessary to operate our Apps. Advertisements shown to children are not personalized, and our advertising SDKs are configured accordingly to limit data collection and ad targeting for this audience. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information beyond what is described here, please contact us at dangpv.work@gmail.com so we can address it.

08Data Retention & Security

We retain information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

09Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to withdraw consent at any time. Residents of the European Economic Area and California may have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA/CPRA, respectively. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dangpv.work@gmail.com.

10Tracking Technologies

Our Apps do not use browser cookies, but advertising and analytics SDKs may use device identifiers that serve a similar purpose, allowing measurement of app performance and ad delivery across sessions.
